Still Driving. Still Building.

I'm Stinson Wakuk.

I'm an OTR truck driver, husband, and father of four.

For years, I believed the answer to financial pressure was simple:

Work more hours.
Take more loads.
Stay out longer.

The problem was that every extra dollar required more of my time.

Eventually, I realized that if I wanted a different future, I needed more than another paycheck. I needed better systems, better habits, and better options.

BuilderShifts is where I document that journey.

Not from a beach.

Not from a stage.

From the driver's seat of a truck.

What I'm Building

  • Better health
  • Emergency savings
  • Financial stability
  • More family time
  • Income not tied entirely to hours worked
  • A future with more options and less stress

Why I Share It Publicly

I don't have everything figured out.

I'm not a financial advisor.

I'm not a guru.

I'm simply documenting what I'm learning while still living it every day.

The wins. The setbacks. The lessons.

If something helps me move forward, I'll share it.

If I make mistakes, I'll share those too.

My hope is that others can learn from both.

Who BuilderShifts Is For

Working people.

Truck drivers.

Parents.

Anyone feeling stuck between rising expenses and limited time.

Anyone trying to improve their health, finances, and future one step at a time.
